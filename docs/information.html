Information

Signal Hill is an audio magazine. It comes out twice a year.

  • Spotify
  • Apple
  • Pocket
    Casts

We publish audio documentaries of all kinds: reporting, essays, shorts, profiles, dispatches, reviews, and other things we haven’t named yet.

You can listen to the latest issue and our archive here on our website, or subscribe on any podcast app to get new issues when they come out. We have a newsletter and social media too.

We run on listener donations, so please support us and our contributors if you can.

If you sign up for a monthly membership, you’ll get access to our special members-only newsletter, and other special stuff. And if you like merch, we have a shop.

Pitch us! We work with a wide range of contributors, many from outside the audio world. Contributors to an issue workshop their stories in collaborative cohorts, with editorial and production support from our team. We pay $500-3000 for stories, depending on their scope.
