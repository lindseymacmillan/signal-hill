Support

Signal Hill is an audio magazine. It comes out twice a year.

Signal Hill is free to listen to, but it’s not free to make. We don’t run ads, we don’t have paywalls, and we don’t make a profit, so we need your support to pay our contributors and editors, and to cover our operating costs.

Behind every story is a months-long process of collaborative workshops and hands-on editing to support every contributor making ambitious work in their own voices – an opportunity that’s really rare for freelancers, at any publication.
